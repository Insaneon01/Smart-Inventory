About Smart Inventory

Smart Inventory is a comprehensive inventory management system designed to help you track your items and their expiration dates efficiently.

Inventory Tracking

Keep track of all your items with detailed information including names, quantities, categories, and expiration dates.

Expiration Alerts

Get notified when items are approaching their expiration dates to minimize waste and ensure freshness.

Smart Scanning

Use camera scanning technology to quickly add items to your inventory with minimal manual input.

Analytics & Insights

View detailed analytics about your inventory patterns and optimize your stock management.
